Brainhack is a collaborative event that focuses on the open sharing of ideas, code, and data. During Brainhack Global, we will meet virtually in Montreal to work collaboratively on projects and learn.

In full brainhack spirit, there will be:

Training

Tutorials, resources, and hands-on sessions for neuroimaging tools and methods.

Projects

Lots of free hacking time to tinker with a project of your choice.

Unconferences

Short sessions to present your research or discuss topics of interest in an informal setting.

Socials

Meet or check-in with experts and peers, share ideas, and plant the seeds of future collaborations.

CoCoLab, Université de Montréal
